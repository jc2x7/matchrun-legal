What is MatchRun?

MatchRun is a mobile app that helps runners find nearby running partners and groups. Users can create or join group runs based on location, pace, and distance. Connecting your Strava account enhances your profile by displaying your recent running activities.

How We Use Your Strava Data

Data we access

  • Your recent running activities (distance, pace, time, elevation)
  • Your Strava athlete ID (to link your account)

How we use it

  • Display your running activities on your MatchRun profile so other runners can see your training history
  • Show a verified runner badge on connected profiles

What we do NOT do

  • We never sell, share, or redistribute your Strava data to third parties
  • We never store your raw activity data permanently — it is fetched on-demand
  • We never post, modify, or delete anything on your Strava account
  • We never compete with or replicate Strava's core features

How to Connect / Disconnect Strava

To connect

  1. Open MatchRun and go to your Profile tab
  2. Tap "Connect Strava" (orange button)
  3. You will be redirected to Strava's website to authorize MatchRun
  4. After authorizing, you will be redirected back to the app automatically

To disconnect

  1. Open MatchRun and go to your Profile tab
  2. Scroll to the Strava section
  3. Tap "Disconnect" — your Strava data will be removed from MatchRun immediately

You can also revoke access directly from Strava: go to strava.com/settings/apps and remove MatchRun.

Troubleshooting

Connection failed or "disallowed_useragent" error

Make sure you are running the latest version of MatchRun. The app opens Safari for Strava login — if you see an error, try again or restart the app.

Activities not showing

Only running activities are displayed. Make sure your Strava activities are set to visible (not private). Try disconnecting and reconnecting your Strava account.

Want to delete your account and data?

Go to Profile → Delete Account in the app. All your data, including Strava tokens, will be permanently removed.
